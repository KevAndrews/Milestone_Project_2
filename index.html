Welcome to the world of Memoria

Long before the world can remember labyrinths and mazes appeared around the world. Brave souls ventured forth to see what lay within these mysterious places. It was subtle at first, people began to forget the names and other words of our world. We soon found to our dismay that when someone was lost to these places a word, a name was lost with them. But not all was lost, we found by defeating the ceatures of these place we would regain what was once lost to us.

As part of Memoria's Labyrinth Guard you are tasked with clearing out the labyrinth of animals. By killing the ceatures here we can return the names of the animals of our world to us.

Instructions

You have 60 seconds to complete the Game.

You can move by left-clicking any adjacent tile but you cannot move diagonally.

You can increase your score by collecting memories from the enemies scattered throughout the level.

Let me know if you find all the memories scattered throughout the game by emailing me using the Contact Me button on the main menu.
